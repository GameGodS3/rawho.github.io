Faqs

Who can participate in BitFlip?

Anyone! Whether you are a total beginner or a tech wizard, it doesn’t matter. Everyone is welcome to participate in BitFlip! :)

How much does it cost to participate in BitFliP?

Zero. Zip. Zilch. Nada. Nothing. Admission to BitFlip is completely free, thanks to our sponsors!

Is it a team event?

Yes, you can have up to 5 members in a team.

Can I apply individually?

Yes, you can also form a team after you get selected.

Does BitFlip have a selection round?

Well yes, but actually no. We plan to have a completely lenient selection process. It all boils down to your idea that you want to work on during the hackathon, so you don’t necessarily need to have fancy GitHub or LinkedIn accounts.

What do I get by attending BitFlip?

Everything! You get to meet your fellow hackers and our wonderful mentors. On top of that, we have some exciting prizes for the winners.

Ummm, hardware tracks?

We sadly don’t have any hardware track, since it’s an online hackathon. Maybe next time :)

Is there a code of conduct?

Yep, we will be following the Devfolio’s Code of Conduct
